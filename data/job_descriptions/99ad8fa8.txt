This internship provides an opportunity to work on real-world product  development, involving all aspects of the software lifecycle — from system design  and development to deployment and maintenance.  Interns will collaborate in an agile environment, contribute to live projects, and  follow structured workflows using Git and GitHub for version control and  collaboration. The role encourages adaptability and cross-platform thinking to  deliver efficient, reliable, and maintainable software systems.  Key Responsibilities  Design, develop, and maintain modules as part of ongoing product development.  Write clean, efficient, and well-documented code.  Collaborate using Git and GitHub for version control and peer collaboration.  Contribute to product discussions, sprint reviews, and technical improvements.  Conduct testing, debugging, and optimization to ensure performance and  reliability.  Maintain consistent communication, documentation, and progress tracking  throughout the internship.
